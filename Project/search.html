Movie Search

When the users open the application, they have the option to search for a certain movie. They can do that by writing the name of the movie on the search frame and then by hitting the search button.
After hitting the button they will be redirected in a page that containes the movie of their choice. There they can see a brief introduction to the plot of the movie.They also have the option to see the user's reviews or add one themselfs.
In the case of a spelling error or wrong input they will be notified with the appropriate message.
