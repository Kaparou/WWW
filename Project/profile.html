Profile Customization

When someone uses our application they will see our main page.
If they have a registered account they will have the option to login. After their login they will see on the top right corner the button "profile". If they press it they will be redirected to the profile page.
In that page they have the options to: Change their profile picture, name, date of birth, gender and email in the Personal info tab.
Change their password.
And last see their favorites list.
Also, they have the option to search other users and see their profile , their favorites list and they can also follow them.
