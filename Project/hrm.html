Highest Rated Movies

In the main page of MoviePedia the users have the option to see the highest rated movies of our database. They can do that by clicking on the "Highest rated movies" tab.
There they can browse through the movies that other users gave the highest rating(Top 10). Next to the image of the movie there is also a brief description. By clicking on the title of the movie they will be redirected to the page of the movie. There they will be able to see the details and possibly add their own review.
