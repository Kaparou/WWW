Description of MoviePedia:

MoviePedia is an online database of information related to films, television programs including cast, production crew, plot summaries, ratings, fan and critical reviews. An additional feature is user accounts. The movie and profile pages of MoviePedia are accessible to all internet users, but a registration process is necessary to contribute information to the site.
